Privacy Policy

At All Car Parts we are committed to protect the privacy of our customers. Thus, as a result, we have created this privacy policy section as in order to clear to our customers how we use the information that our customers provide us through the website.

Below are some of the questions which may surely come in your while filling data that we request for:

  • What kind of data do we collect and how we use that?
  • How we protect the data?
  • Do we share your data with other organizations?
  • What are cookies and how they are utilized by us?
  • Does the customer can change or update the information they had provided?

What kind of data do we collect and how we use that?

General
The data that customers provide to us is used for:

  • Allow our customers to online purchase the products offered by us
  • Offer our customer with some personalized web content that tailors specific need of the customers
  • Consistently enhance the functionality and convenience of our website
  • For some market analysis and different other internal things
  • To cater our customers with the promotions offered by us and our partners
Account Setup

When you set up your account, you are required to provide your email address as well as password along with the information of your vehicle. The information that you provide to us through your account provides us to give you personalize searches facilitate the process of ordering. During the process of registration, we will ask your address, name, email address and other personal data along with data of your vehicle. The more information you correctly provide the better experience you will get.

Ordering

At All Car Parts we make use of both email and shopping carts forms to customers to request information, services, and products. We may also gather customer contact information (such as email address) and financial data (like debit or credit card information). This data enables us to process as well as fulfill the orders of our customers and notify them with their order status. When you provide your credit card information through an online form, you are being connected with a secure server that encrypts the information over the internet.

Surveys and Promotions

Occasionally, we request our customers for some contests or surveys. Participating in these contests or surveys is optional and customers have a choice to disclose or keep the information confidential. The information which we take involve contact information (like shipping address and name), and demographic information (like age, zip code or income range). The contact information we take will be used to notify the winners and award prizes.

The survey information is utilized for purpose of checking or enhancing the use and experience of the website. Moreover, we will utilize this information to personalize the experience of customers for this website. Moreover, this data of customers is used for some internal purposes such as market analysis.

Financial Data

The financial information which we gather is only utilized for billing of services and products and not for any other reason. We do not store any credit card data on the server or system.

Mailing Lists

The contact information that we take while you fill registration form, contest form, order form or from other sources is utilized to send promotional emails to our customers. Moreover, this contact information is shared with some of our selected partners who want to get connected with our customers. The users have the choice to opt out for receiving future updates by checking the box while modifying or opening the account.

How we protect the data?

Passwords
Passwords We are committed to provide customers with high-security level. So, to check your personal information you need to enter a password that you created. We suggest our customers not to share their password with anyone.

Financial Information and Registration

All the personal information that you provide to us that includes name, address, contact number, credit card information are stored in a safe database. We make use of the best practices of an industry so as to ensure that our customer's data is secure.

Secure server

Whenever you place the order through our website, you need to transmit your order data such as credit card number on a secure checkout page. Our secure server makes use of SSL software which allows us to get the information which your web browser already encrypts before transmitting. Thus, all the information that you cater to us will be transmitted with the use of the secure connection. The browser of customers that do not support this security level is not allowed to order any products from us.

Display of Credit Card Information

We understand privacy is one of the main concerns of customers. This is the reason we never show more than five digit number of your credit card on screen or in invoices generated.

Do we share your data with other organizations?

General

At All Car Parts we never share our customer's personal information with any third party. But we may share your information with some reliable and trustworthy third parties such as localAll Car Parts stores such that they can send you services and products offers as per your interest. The users have the option to opt out from receiving the emails after checking the appropriate box while modifying or opening the account.

Aggregate Statistics

We offer aggregate statistics about our customers traffic patterns, sales, and other related data to the third party. Well, this data does not include your personal information.

Account Information

We may sometimes release account information if it is necessary for:

  • As per standard law
  • Apply provisions of our conditions and terms

Verification of Credit Card

Whenever you place any order from us, we will take help of credit card Verification Company to check credit card and other information that you provide before we proceed with your order. We have made an agreement with such organizations to make sure that the personal information of our customers remains confidential. Moreover, there is another safeguard in billing data you offer for your credit card which is used to verify that you are the only owner of that card.

What are cookies and how they are utilized by us?

The cookies are the kind of data which our website sends to your browser and later it is stored on the hard drive of your computer so that we can recognize you whenever you return. Every page on which you logged in or they are customizable need to accept cookies. We make use of ads too on our site which contains cookies. The cookies by us also contain personal information such as email address which enables us to offer the features such as storing of items in shopping cart among creating and visit the personalized site as per your specific automobile. Most of the individual's web browser accepts cookies automatically, but they can change their browser settings to prevent it.

Does the customer can change or update the information they had provided?

The customer is allowed to access their personal information anytime. They can update it, modify it, alter or delete the data they have provided us. For this, access your account page of a website with email and password.
